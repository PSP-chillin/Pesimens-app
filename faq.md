# FAQ

## What is PESiMENs?

A private, polished campus super-app for PES University students. It covers academics, social features, career tools, games, and more — all in one installable app.

## Where can I find the app?

The live product is at [pesimens.app](https://pesimens.app).

## What platforms can I install it on?

PESiMENs is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on Android, iOS, and desktop via the browser's "Add to Home Screen" or "Install" prompt — no app store required.

## How do I sign in?

There are three options:
- **PESU Academy login** — use your SRN and PESU Academy password.
- **Google Auth** — sign in securely with your Google account.
- **Email magic link** — receive a one-time sign-in link to your registered email address.

## Is this repository open source?

No. PESiMENs is a private, closed-source product. This public repository is a transparency and product showcase page — it does not contain any source code.

## Does this repo contain passwords or API keys?

No. Absolutely no passwords, API keys, service credentials, or live secrets are present here.

## What is Athena AI?

Athena is the built-in AI assistant inside PESiMENs. It helps with study questions, campus navigation, and exam insights.

## Are confessions really anonymous?

Yes. Confessions are fully anonymous — your SRN or profile is never linked to anything you post in the confessions feature. Anonymity is enforced at the data level, not just the display level.

## Is the PESU Academy sync safe?

Yes. Your PESU Academy credentials are encrypted before use and are never stored in plaintext.

## Can someone rebuild the app from this repo?

No. This repo is intentionally minimal and does not include any application source code, backend logic, or database schema.
