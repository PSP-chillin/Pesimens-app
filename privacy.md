# Privacy Policy

PESiMENs is designed with a privacy-first approach. This document describes the platform's privacy principles at a high level.

## What we may collect

- Account and profile details needed to operate the app (name, email, campus, avatar)
- Community content created by users (PYQs, confessions, messages, stories)
- Basic usage and security telemetry needed to keep the service reliable

## What we do not collect

- Institutional academic records such as grades, marksheets, or official exam results
- Passwords — PESU Academy credentials are encrypted and never stored in plaintext
- The identity of confession authors — confessions are fully anonymous. A student's SRN or profile is never linked to any confession they post. Anonymity is enforced at the data level.

## Why we collect data

- To provide core app features
- To support moderation and abuse prevention
- To maintain account access and secure sessions
- To improve product reliability and user experience

## How data is protected

- Sensitive access is restricted to production systems and trusted operators
- Security controls are used to reduce unauthorized access and abuse
- User-generated content is moderated where needed
- No operational secrets or credentials are stored in this repository

## Data retention

- Data is retained only as long as needed for the product to operate
- Security and audit data may be kept for operational or compliance reasons
- Deleted accounts and removed content follow the product's internal retention process

## Third-party services

The live app uses third-party services for hosting, authentication, AI inference, notifications, and payments. Each of those services maintains its own privacy policy.
